Opencode

An Opensource Event

Opencode is a month-long open source event. All undergraduates and postgraduates around the world can participate in developing open sourced projects and contributing in them.

Opencode aims to raise awareness about open source software development among the students and developers around the world.

  • Date:January
  • Duration: 1 Month
  • Prizes: Cash Prize, Stickers, T-Shirt
